Welcome to the Number Challenge Game!

Test your mathematical skills in this exciting game where you aim to reach a target number using basic operations and strategic thinking.

Game Basics

1

Understand the Goal

You'll be given 6 starting numbers and a 3-digit target number. Your goal is to reach the target number using the starting numbers and basic mathematical operations.

2

Use Operations

Combine the starting numbers using addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to reach the target number. You can only use each starting number once.

3

Optimize Your Solution

Try to reach the target number in the shortest time possible and with the minimum number of operations. The more efficient your solution, the higher your score!

4

Submit Your Answer

Once you've found a way to reach the target number, submit your solution to see how well you did and earn points for the leaderboard.

Game Modes

Practice

Hone your skills with practice problems of varying difficulty levels.

Challenge

Test yourself with a series of fixed questions that progressively increase in difficulty. This mode is designed to help you adapt to the game's challenges and improve your problem-solving skills step by step.

Online

Compete against other players in real-time challenges. As you solve problems, you'll collect points that are displayed on daily and monthly leaderboards, allowing you to track your progress and compare your performance with other players.
